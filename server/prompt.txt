*   **Versatility:** Carbon is the building block of life and many materials we use because of two special properties:
    *   **Tetravalency:** Carbon can form four bonds with other atoms.
    *   **Catenation:** The amazing ability of carbon atoms to link together to form long chains, branches, or rings.
*   **Bonding:** Carbon forms **covalent bonds** (sharing electrons) with itself and other elements like hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, and sulfur. It can form single, double, or triple bonds.
*   **Homologous Series:** These are groups of compounds with similar structures and the same **functional group** (like alcohols or ketones) attached to carbon chains of varying lengths.
*   **Daily Applications:** Carbon compounds are vital as fuels, and substances like ethanol and ethanoic acid are key industrial chemicals.
