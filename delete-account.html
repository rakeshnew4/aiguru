Delete My Account

You can request permanent deletion of your AiGuru account and all associated data at any time. Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

⚠️ Deletion is immediate and irreversible. All your data listed below will be removed permanently.

Data that will be deleted:

  • Your profile (name, email, grade, school)
  • All subjects and chapters you created
  • All chat and tutoring history
  • Quiz scores and progress data
  • Subscription and payment records
  • Referral code and earned bonuses

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the AiGuru app on your Android device.
  2. Tap your name or Profile on the Home screen.
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the warning dialog. You will be asked to sign in with Google once more to verify your identity.
  5. Your account and all data will be permanently deleted immediately.

Don't have access to the app? Email us and we'll process the deletion manually within 7 business days:

support@aiguru.in

Please include the email address associated with your account in your message.
